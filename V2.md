# V2 — Implementation Log

Living doc for V2, same shape as V1.md. Every checkpoint (see [ROADMAP.md](./ROADMAP.md) for the checklist) gets one section here, filled in **as it's built**:

- **Questions asked & decisions** — anything that had more than one reasonable answer, what was chosen, and why
- **What was built** — the concrete files/features
- **Why** — the reasoning behind how it was built, not just what
- **How** — how to run/verify it
- **Notes / gotchas** — anything that cost time or is easy to get wrong again later

ARCHITECTURE.md's "Job sourcing strategy (V2+)" section is the *target* design for V2 (provider list, the `JobListing` adapter pattern, why Greenhouse/Lever were dropped); this file is the *actual* build record, checkpoint by checkpoint, once Checkpoint 9 starts.
